Placement Process Timeline

Registration

Students register for the placement drive through our portal. Detailed guidelines and deadlines are shared with all registered students.

Pre-Placement Talk

Companies deliver presentations to inform students about job roles, work culture, and growth opportunities within their organizations.

Aptitude Test

An online test is conducted to evaluate logical reasoning, quantitative skills, and domain knowledge of the candidates.

Group Discussion

Shortlisted candidates participate in group discussions to assess communication skills, teamwork, and problem-solving abilities.

Personal Interview

Final interviews are conducted by company representatives to evaluate technical and behavioral skills of the candidates.

Offer Letters

Successful candidates receive offer letters and guidance on the next steps to join their respective companies.
